Since the early 2000's, I've been involved in many other open-source projects, ranging from Eclipse projects to Apache ones. This led me, in October 2010, to join SonarSource as an Engineer first and then as a Product Manager. I'm now taking care of the Product Management team at SonarSource.

Since the early 2000's, I also started worrying about the state of our planet, and I've been doing many things over the past 20 years to help protect our environment. Recently, I joined Time for the Planet as an associate: we leverage collective intelligence and an open-source model to push innovations forward to fight climate change.
